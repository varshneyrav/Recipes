Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji, originating from the streets of Mumbai, has gained fans worldwide for its wholesome blend of veggies in a tangy tomato gravy, paired with soft Pav. This versatile dish can be enjoyed at any timeâ€”breakfast, lunch, dinner, or as a snack. Historically, Pav Bhaji was created in the 1850s to provide textile mill workers with a quick, affordable, and satisfying meal during their short lunch breaks. A local vendor combined leftovers from other dishes into a spicy bhaji, substituting traditional rice or roti with Pav, giving birth to this beloved dish.
